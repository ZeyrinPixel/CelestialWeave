**Celestial Weave** is a world generation mod for Hytale that overhauls the landscape into a surreal, interconnected reality. 

---

### ⚠️ Early Development Phase
Please note that this mod is currently a **Work In Progress**.
It currently only features a few custom biomes and portals to reach the world, everything is subject to change.
Performance is not fully optimized, so expect slow loading when entering the dimension.

---

## __Instructions__
1. Place .zip in Hytale mods folder
2. Enable mod on world
3. Celesital Weave Portal and Return Portal can be found in the Arcane crafting bench or creative inventory

---

## __Current Features__
### Items:
- **Broken Portal:**
  - Acquired by mining an existing Forgotten Temple Gateway, Celestial Weave Portal, or Return Portal.
- **Celestial Weave Portal:**
  - Crafted from a Broken Portal, 6 Ropes, and one of each Essences.
  - Use to enter the Celestial Weave
- **Return Portal:**
  - Crafted from a Broken Portal and a resource found in each of the Celestial Weave biomes.
  - Use to return to your original world (default world)
### Locations:
- **Celestial Loom:**
  - The remnants of a massive loom once capable of weaving dimensions together.
### Biomes:
- **Resting Giants:**
  - A vibrant, surreal overworld where lush vegetation grows and large, pale "spinal" structures cut through the main landscape.
  - Descend beneath the colorful surface into massive, treacherous caverns that offer a stark, nightmarish contrast to the beauty above.
- **Hanging Grove:**
  - An inverted forest where large, gravity-defying trees grow downwards to form a dense canopy floor.
- **Whispering Dunes:**
  - A vast expanse of golden dunes, where the winds carry whispers of ancient civilizations and sand formations shift to reveal hidden ruins. (Sounds/Structures not fully implemented)
- **Salt Roughs:**
  - Water seeps out of the cracks in a landscape of towering salt pillars. Rare luminescent gems grow scattered throughout the terrain, forming in the mineral-rich waters.
- **Voidlands:**
  - A decaying archipelago of fractured islands drifting aimlessly over the void.

*More biomes and structures are planned for future updates. Feedback is welcome!*
