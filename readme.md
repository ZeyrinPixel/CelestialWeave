**Celestial Weave** is a world generation mod for Hytale that overhauls the landscape into a surreal, interconnected reality. 

---

### ⚠️ Early Development Phase
Please note that this mod is currently a **Work In Progress**. It currently only features a few custom biomes, and everything is subject to change.

---

## __Instructions__
1. Place .zip in Hytale mods folder
2. Enable mod on world
3. Be op (`/op self`)
4. Activate World Gen V2 (`/world settings worldgentype set HytaleGenerator`)
5. Reload world (`/worldgen reload --clear`)

---

## __Current Features__
### Biomes:
- **Resting Giants:**
  - A vibrant, surreal overworld where lush vegetation grows and large, pale "spinal" structures cut through the main landscape.
  - Descend beneath the colorful surface into massive, treacherous caverns that offer a stark, nightmarish contrast to the beauty above.
- **Whispering Dunes:**
  - A vast expanse of golden dunes, where the winds carry whispers of ancient civilizations and sand formations shift to reveal hidden ruins. (Sounds/Structures not fully implemented)
- **Salt Roughs**
  - Water seeps out of the cracks in a landscape of towering salt pillars. Rare luminescent gems grow scattered throughout the terrain, forming in the mineral-rich waters.
- **Voidlands:**
  - A decaying archipelago of fractured islands drifting aimlessly over the void.

*More biomes and structures are planned for future updates. Feedback is welcome!*
